About Developer Student Clubs

Developer Student Clubs is a Google Developers program for university students from any academic background in their undergraduate or graduate term to learn mobile and web development skills, design thinking skills and leadership skills. By joining a DSC, students build their professional and personal network and work together to build solutions for local problems in a peer-to-peer learning environment.

What We Do

We are forming the DSC team for AY 2020/2021. We thrive to provide opportunities to learn & access deep technical content by hosting cool training workshops, collaborating with amazing community members, organizing tech talks and various conferences. We primarily aim at making use of Google Technologies to empower the audience around us. If you are passionate about using technology to make a positive impact, apply to join us in this journey to empower Tech-To-All. We would love to welcome students from all areas of expertise be it Technical, Marketing and Public Relations, Development and Design or Creatives and Production.

We're on a Mission

Education

Through this Google platform, every member of the club gets an opportunity to learn about a wide range of technical topics where new skills are gained through hands-on workshops, project building activities and training provided by the team and various collaborations.

Collaboration

We welcome everyone, including those with diverse backgrounds and different majors to collaborate and co-create new innovative ideas that would help us connect to more students interested in the developer technologies. Thereby building solutions for the local problems and begetting a community that aspires for a positive and impactful change.

Innovation

Through education and collaboration we want to provide you with a platform where you can innovate and experiment along with us. Developing innovative products is what separates you from the crowd. Therefore, we want to lay the foundation necessary to bring our innovative visions to life through the power of technology.

Motivation

Our main motivation is to be able to inspire students and engage members to learn as well as share their best practices with one another so that all us, as a community, are one step closer to spreading technology and continuing to be the change that we seek.
